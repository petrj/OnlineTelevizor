Privacy policy agreement for application Online Televizor




Privacy of its users is for android application Online Televizor extremely important. All processed data are considered strictly confidential and are handled in accordance with the law.

What is purpose of application?

Purpose of application is watching TV and radio through czech IPTV services on android devices such mobile phones, TVs and tablets.
User can record just playing stream to file, records are only for personal purposes and author of the application is not responsible for copyright law violation.

What informations are collected?

Application collects only such data, that is necessary to access APIs of supported czech IPTVs (SledovaniTV, Kuki, O2 TV). This data is not transferred anywhere and the author of the application does not have access to it.

What are application permissions?

  • Internet access
  • Request ignore battery optimization
  • Google Play billing service
